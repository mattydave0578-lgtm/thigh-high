THE THIGH
MANIFESTO

It started with an argument. Jayden and Lerato were grilling at Jayden's flat in Salt River, and someone had the nerve to serve a breast fillet burger. Dry. Flavourless. An insult to the craft. That night, they made a pact: never again.

Chicken thighs are superior — more fat, more flavour, more moisture. They can take the heat of the grill, the soak of a brine, the weight of every topping you throw at them. They don't dry out. They don't give up. They're built different.

The pickles came next. Not the sad, limp pickles you find at chain restaurants. We source and brine our own — thick-cut, punchy, with just enough heat to make you reach for the next bite. They cut through the richness of the thigh perfectly.

And then there's the house mayo. Lerato's grandmother's recipe, tweaked obsessively over two years. Egg yolks, a hint of toasted garlic, white wine vinegar, and something we'll never tell you. It ties everything together. It's the reason people drive from Stellenbosch.

🍗
THIGHS ONLY

We auditioned the breast. We fired it after day one. The thigh delivers more flavour per bite, stays juicy under pressure, and handles bold seasoning without flinching. There is no other option.

🥒
THE PICKLE OBSESSION

We brine our own pickles in-house. Thick slices, fermented just long enough to develop that deep, funky tang. Every burger gets a generous handful — not a suggestion of pickles, but a commitment.

✨
THE HOUSE MAYO

Made fresh every morning. Never from a bottle. It's rich, garlicky, slightly tangy, and absolutely the best thing you'll taste all week. We put it on everything, and we're not sorry.

// the craft

HOW WE DO IT

There are no shortcuts in this kitchen. Here's what happens before it reaches your hands.

01
🍗
THE THIGH SELECTION

We source free-range chicken thighs daily from a small farm outside Paarl. They arrive fresh every morning — never frozen. We trim, clean, and prep each one by hand. Size matters: we pick for consistency so every bite is the same amount of perfection.

02
🧂
THE BRINE

Each thigh spends at least 12 hours in our buttermilk and herb brine. This isn't optional — it's the difference between a good burger and a life-altering one. The brine tenderises, seasons to the bone, and sets up that crackling crust when it hits the fryer.

03
🥒
THE PICKLE PROGRAMME

We ferment our own pickles in-house, three different batches running at any time: classic dill, spicy peri-peri, and bread-and-butter. They're cut thick — none of that wafer-thin nonsense — and they go on every single burger without exception.

04
✨
THE MAYO RITUAL

The house mayo is made from scratch every single morning. Egg yolks from the same farm as our chickens, a splash of white wine vinegar, toasted garlic, and the secret ingredient we will take to our graves. It takes 20 minutes. We do it every day. No compromises.

05
🔥
THE FRY

Double-dredged in seasoned flour and fried in fresh sunflower oil at exactly 175°C. We check the temperature every 30 minutes — not every hour, not when we remember. The result is a shatteringly crispy exterior and a thigh so juicy it practically steams when you bite into it.

06
🍔
THE BUILD

Bottom bun gets mayo. Then the thigh. Then the pickles — generous, always. Then whatever else the burger calls for. Then more mayo on the top bun. Every burger is assembled to order, wrapped immediately, and out the window in under 4 minutes. No sitting under a lamp.
